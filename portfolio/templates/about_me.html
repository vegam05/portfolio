My Journey in Tech

Hi there! Welcome to my About Me page. Let me tell you a bit about myself and my journey in the world of technology.

Early Coding Explorations:

My fascination with coding began at a surprisingly young age - nine years old! Back then, my explorations were like dipping my toes into the vast ocean of programming. I dabbled in HTML, learning the basics of web page structure, and tinkered with Visual Basic, taking my first steps into the world of application development.

Finding my passion:

As I progressed through my academic journey, particularly after high school, the path I wanted to take became undeniably clear. My passion for technology intensified, and I knew I had to pursue my career in this exciting field.

Solidifying My Foundation:

Over time, I solidified my proficiency in C and C++. These languages provided a robust foundation in programming principles, teaching me essential concepts like memory management and low-level control.

The Allure of Python:

However, in recent times, it's Python that has truly captured my focus. Its elegance and flexibility have captivated me. It's a powerful language that not only enhances my programming skills but also unlocks the fascinating world of machine learning and deep learning.
